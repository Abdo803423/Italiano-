Our Story

The Italiano
Legacy

Since 1999, Italiano has been the heart of authentic Italian dining in Fayoum. Our recipes are passed down through generations — each dish a love letter to the traditions of Naples, Rome, and Tuscany.

We source only the finest ingredients: imported DOP mozzarella, hand-rolled pasta, and fresh herbs from our own garden. Every bite tells a story of passion and craftsmanship.
